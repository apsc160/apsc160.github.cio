Optional zyBooks Text

Traditional textbooks don't work for everyone. A zyBook is an online and interactive textbook with less text but more action. It mixes learning activities such as question sets and animations with short written content. If you are interested in trying it out, we’ve prepared one for this course. Please note that its use is entirely optional. You can try the first chapter for free before deciding whether or not to purchase it.

To purchase and access the book:

  1. Create an account at http://learn.zybooks.com
  2. Enter zyBook code `UBCAPSC16017W2`
  3. Subscribe

A subscription is $58 (USD) and will last until May 14, 2018. Students will be able to subscribe until Apr 14, 2018.

The online nature of zyBook encourages you to try things out and gives you immediate feedback. Initial research shows interactive content improves students’ engagement and performance.
